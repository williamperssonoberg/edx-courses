Introduction to Death Metal

William is a Swedish dude, hooked on Death Metal in all it's glorious forms. He also loves black metal, thrash metal, heavy metal and many more smaller subgenres, but the riff-centered, old school death metal takes the proverbial cake!

OSDM

OSDM stands for Old School Death Metal.

OSDM has always had a strong scene in the USA, but was further developed in Europe, specifically in Sweden, the UK, the Netherlands, and Germany. Swedish Old School Death Metal (specifically the bands originating from around the Stockholm scene) is known for it's heavy use of the BOSS HM2 pedal guitar effect pedal.

History

The first death metal bands are usually credited as DEATH from Florida, and POSSESSED from San Francisco (who released a demo called "Death Metal" in 1984). Both started in the early 80's as a progression of the faster and darker thrash metal bands of the time (bands such as Slayer, Metallica, and Venom - to name a few).

Features

What the HECK is death metal all about?

Death Metal is defined by it's down tuned string intstruments; usually one or two guitars and a bass guitar, as well as growl vocals and high intensity drumming.
